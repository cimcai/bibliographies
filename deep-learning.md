### Early deep learning: Ivakhnenko & Lapa

- Ivakhnenko, A. G., & Lapa, V. G. (1965/1966). *Cybernetic Predicting Devices.* First working deep learners with many layers. https://gwern.net/doc/ai/1966-ivakhnenko.pdf

- Ivakhnenko, A. G. (1968). The group method of data handling; a rival of the method of stochastic approximation. *Soviet Automatic Control, 13*, 43–55. https://gmdhsoftware.com/GMDH_%20Anastasakis_and_Mort_2001.pdf

- Ivakhnenko, A. G. (1971). Polynomial theory of complex systems. *IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man and Cybernetics, 4*, 364–378. Classic deep learning with 8-layer polynomial nets. https://www.knowledgeminer.eu/pdf/Ivakhnenko_Polynomial.pdf

### Backpropagation and gradient-based learning

- Linnainmaa, S. (1976). The representation of the cumulative rounding error of an algorithm as a Taylor expansion of the local rounding errors. *BIT, 16*, 146–160. First explicit publication of modern reverse-mode automatic differentiation (backpropagation). https://papers.baulab.info/papers/also/Linnainmaa-1976.pdf

- Schmidhuber, J. (2014). Who invented backpropagation? Historical overview and credit assignment for backpropagation and its precursors. https://www.idsia.ch/~juergen/who-invented-backpropagation.html

- Kelley, H. J. (1960). Gradient theory of optimal flight paths. *ARS Journal, 30*(10), 947–954. Classic precursor of modern backpropagation in optimal control. https://perceptrondemo.com/assets/1960-kelley-07dff188.pdf

- Lemaréchal, C. (2012). Cauchy and the gradient method. In *Optimization Stories* (Documenta Mathematica Extra Volume). Historical review of gradient descent. https://www.zib.de/userpage/groetschel/publications/OptimizationStories.pdf

- Amari, S. I. (1967). A theory of adaptive pattern classifiers. *IEEE Transactions on Electronic Computers, EC-16*(3), 279–307. Early use of stochastic gradient descent for multilayer networks. https://people.idsia.ch/~juergen/amari1967.pdf

- Tsypkin, Y. Z. (1966). Adaptation, training and self-organization automatic control systems. *Avtomatika i Telemekhanika, 27*, 23–61. Early work on gradient-descent-based online learning for nonlinear systems. https://people.idsia.ch/~juergen/deep-learning-history.html

- Tsypkin, Y. Z. (1971). *Adaptation and Learning in Automatic Systems.* Academic Press. Monograph on adaptation and learning in nonlinear systems. https://shop.elsevier.com/books/adaptation-and-learning-in-automatic-systems/tsypkin/978-0-12-702050-1
